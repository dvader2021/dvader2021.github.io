Goodbye Dear

Published: Tue 08 September 2020
By Subu

In poetry.

tags: sep 2020 birthday inspiration romance farewell events

Goodbye my dear
Goodbye forever

As you walk away into oblivion
Fade away to become a memory
Stepping into a whole new world
Faraway from me
I stand here reminiscing the many years of romance
Delicately hidden between love and hate
Goodbye dear
Goodbye forever

You are there in the eyes, my tears
Your laughter pervades my life
Your smell lingers everywhere
You remain etched in me, my life
Now and forever
I stand here listening to my heartbeat
Delicately rhythm between tears and laughter
Goodbye dear
Goodbye forever

At this juncture
As you walk alone into unknown
I ask the wind to be gentle
so not to disturb you
I ask the storm to be mellow
so not to drench you
I stand watching the girl who gave meaning to my life
Delicately straddle the land between light and shadow
Goodbye dear
Goodbye forever

Goodbye my dear
Goodbye forever
