Life..

< 1 minute read   published Nov 2017

Looking back I see, Life has taken its own colorful path
Starting in a very familiar and similar way
We find our self-growing and flowing
taking very striking and dissimilar way

Few gained a family to be proud of
Others treading a long lonely path
Myriad ways to live and hope
To wake up in a better new day and bath

Few gained riches big to fathom
Others lost everything and bottom
Many others struggled to buy the ordinary cotton
Dreaming of riches that were fresh not rotten

Few lived healthy
Along With their generations thriving without a cry
Some died early
Leaving behind unfinished business for others to pry
many struggled with health
No matter what food exercise or advice they did try

The paths were many and complex
The dreams simple but even more perplex
The end remained the same for all to flex
No matter how, what or why you say Rex

context/back story: A look back at the lives of my generation; where we started and where we are at this point in time
