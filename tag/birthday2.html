A Monster called Neelu

summary:

a fiction created to delicately scare my little child when he refused to calm down

A monster called Neelu
With green eyes, red hair and pink hue

Wearing a long hat of purple & blue
His eyes gleaming with glitter and mysterious dew

A long sleeved shirt fitted to look crumpled but new
His ill-fitted trousers were draped looking forced to hew

His long shabby beard tied with a bandana flue,
And a ring on a nose, he looked someone with zero clue

Hissing like a snake and murmur so low and blue
He was totally silent with an occasional phew!

With a cane in one hand and broken chain for crew
He walked with a limp and a smile so few

With an evil look in his eyes and hoarse voice to mew
He was the monster of the twilight to scare children like yew

In the dark alley of the night, with nothing to chew
Amidst howling winds, he simmered in the moonlight brew

He strode with a purpose in the midnight blue
To scare the children and drink the rabbit stew

A monster called Neelu
With green eyes, red hair and pink hue
