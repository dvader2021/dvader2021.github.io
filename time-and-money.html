Time and Money

published on 2021-01-01    1 minute read

Time and money
entangled in a complex knot
what is valuable? You ask
a question that is not easy to unclot

I have 24 hours every day you say
Is it real? I mime
Many tasks to perform to run the day
Yet we waste valuable time rushing to save a dime

Looking around
You see a few, buy back their time
By spending their dollars
For others to do their crime

Why cannot I do the same? You ask
Soon realizing you need a job
to pay the bill and enjoy the fun
So, you sell your time to earn the cob

If you plan early,
One day you can change the game
With enough dough coming your way
You can buy your time back without any shame

Money is a powerful tool
Can work diligently for you everyday
Don’t put in on a pedestal
To change its haven or bay
