48

1 minute read   published 2009-08-15

The angel paused briefly and smiled pensively
Giving me 48 hours to live my life fully
Letting me know, she was not doing this willingly
But a higher order has decided my life for me seemingly

The directive struck me as a thunderbolt
Slowly sinking within, giving my heart a jolt
As I staggered in the darkness to climb this Holt
I had the uneasy feeling of a fallen molt

Not willing to lose this foolish game
I planned to drink the priciest scotch insane
Eat the juiciest steak with no shame
And party with the choicest friends and dame

Wanting to spend these precious times
I turned around to see no one here mine
Their life moved on minus me all fine
Hanging me with no balance in my day pristine

Then it stuck again, I leave no legacy
Nothing whatsoever that someone will miss me or my ecstasy
Mundane was my dreams, so puerile
Something I need to do, to touch at least one life, one smile

Dawn broke and I awoke with joy and pain
I was not dead but happy to be born again
A new life that was gifted me for someone to gain
To leave a mark and touch some life, make some rain
