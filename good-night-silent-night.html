Good Night Silent Night

summary:

my take on a classic children's poem

Good night silent night
Sleep tight, may the bugs never bite
Off you go, into the world out of sight

The fairy land where there’s fun and no fright
Here you can play all night long in the bright light
With water, ice, funny mice or a magic kite

Zoom in this dream world as your right
With Superman accompanying in his full might
Waiting to jump on the creepy monsters and fight
Thomas and tank engine buddies always in your sight
To tour the sesame street and Power rangers flight
As Spiderman watches you from his height
Batman takes you on a ride as you sit tight
It is a perfect play area for your night
With your friends Will, Kenny and Simon Zite

It is a wonderful world with no spite
where you are a child not black or white
Good night silent night
Mihir Sleep tight, May the bugs never ever bite
