Hair

< 1 minute read   published 1999-07-31

On the top of the head,
You stand like a crown
Looking at the world
with a smile and a frown

You adorn the person
like the crowning glory
Better than the best jewel,
perfect like a blazing fury

You make the person
own the world with your omen,
Pushing them to the pit
on the day it is bad-hair demon

One who lacks you
Pines for you like a forlorn lover
yet you make them cry, Pitiless
Are you like the withering flower

Out at the barbers,
You wear a pathetic look
Right from the cerebral top
You are swept to the dark nook!!
